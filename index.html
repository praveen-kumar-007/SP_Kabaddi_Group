Our Story

SP Kabaddi Group was founded in 2015 with a passion for promoting the ancient sport of Kabaddi. What started as a small community initiative has now grown into a full-fledged organization with players, coaches, and enthusiasts from all walks of life.

Our journey began with a simple idea - to create a platform where Kabaddi players could showcase their talent and where fans could enjoy the excitement of the sport. Over the years, we have organized numerous tournaments, training camps, and community events to spread awareness about Kabaddi.

Our Mission

At SP Kabaddi Group, we are committed to:

  • Promoting Kabaddi as a mainstream sport
  • Providing quality training to aspiring players
  • Organizing competitive tournaments
  • Creating a community of Kabaddi enthusiasts
  • Supporting talented players to reach their full potential
